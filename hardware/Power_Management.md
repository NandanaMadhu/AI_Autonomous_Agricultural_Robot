# 🔋 Power Management

The AgriVision robot is powered by a rechargeable battery pack that supplies electrical power to all onboard components.

# Estimated Power Consumption

| Component | Approximate Consumption |
|-----------|------------------------:|
| Raspberry Pi 4 | 5–7 W |
| Camera Module | 1 W |
| Ultrasonic Sensor | <0.1 W |
| Bluetooth | Integrated |
| DC Motors | 15–25 W |

---

# Design Considerations

- Stable voltage supply
- Motor isolation from controller
- Rechargeable power source
- Future support for solar charging
- Safe battery operation
